SELF DEFENSE


Why is Self-Defense Important?

1. Safety: The primary importance of self-defence is for women to be able to protect themselves against anything that’s unacceptable in terms of social conduct.

2. Confidence: There is nothing more empowering than having the confidence to analyse a dangerous situation and take actions to overcome them effectively.

3. Empowerment: By encouraging women to handle challenging and dangerous situations on their own, it empowers them to live a healthy and fulfilling life.

4. It instils discipline, which carries over to all other facets of life. It enables women to become more composed, perceptive, adaptable, gain control over their bodies and minds, be more responsive than reactive, be more observant, and develop cognitive awareness.

5. Lessens Dependency: You shouldn't be required to travel with a companion just because you're a woman! Learning self-defense gives you independence and enables you to defend yourself in any circumstance.

6. Health and Fitness: Self-defense classes are a fun way to work out and develop a healthy body, enhancing both physical and mental well-being.

7. Reduces Discrimination/Sexism: Crimes against women can be curbed in a significant way when women are empowered to fight back against any discrimination and sexism.
